Workflows Community Summit (Jan 13)

In collaboration with the workflowsRI project we are organizing the Workflows Community Summit. This unique event will gather a select group of lead researchers from distinct workflow management systems, and will seek to identify crucial challenges in the workflow community. This event will be the first of a series of focused meetings, and will serve to define the strategic goals for upcoming meetings with the science and workflow developer communities.
